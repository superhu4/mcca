Workshops

Fear of God – Simon Boenaidi - Is God supposed to be scary? The Bible shows that God isn’t trying to scare us into believing him. Instead, Exodus chapter one shows us that fearing God gives us life-giving courage. Let’s explore together how the Bible helps us rethink beliefs that may not be helping us.

The Bible Journey: How to Read the Bible – Justin Young – Because the Bible is a big book with a big message, we may be so overwhelmed that we don’t know where to start. Join us as we take small steps in learning how to understand this big book.
