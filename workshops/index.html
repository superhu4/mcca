Workshops

Living by Faith - Gary House (MS 125) – We may want to stand firm in the faith, but in some situations we don’t know how. We will look into the Scriptures to understand what faith is, and learn practical ways to trust God in all kinds of situations.

Why Should We Keep On Singing? – Simon Boenaidi (MS 127) – With the growing concern over depression and anxiety in our generation, why should we keep on singing? We’ll take a look at how the Bible affirms and understand our concern yet offer a different reason to keep on singing in anxious times.

Standing Firm Against Anxiety – Justin Young (MS 129) – Struggling with anxiety can feel overwhelming, but you’re not alone. In this workshop, we’ll explore what Scripture says about anxiety and discover practical steps so that the peace of God will guard your hearts and minds in Christ Jesus.

Standing Firm Against Frustration & Anger - Kirt Thallman(MS 141) - We all get frustrated and angry. Even Jesus did and threw over some tables in the Temple. Learn what type of anger person you are. Why you get angry. And how to deal with the frustration.

Standing Firm in the Chaos: Practical Habits to Grow in a Busy Life – Corey Bourassa ( ) - Ever feel like growing your faith is another item on an already overflowing to-do list? In this workshop, we'll explore the Bible's wisdom on spiritual growth and explore practical, everyday habits of grace to foster a deeper connection with Christ. Join us to discover how you can begin today to intentionally grow into the person God desires you to be.
