Workshops

Fear of God (OH114) – Simon Boenaidi - Is God supposed to be scary? The Bible shows that God isn’t trying to scare us into believing him. Instead, Exodus chapter one shows us that fearing God gives us life-giving courage. Let’s explore together how the Bible helps us rethink beliefs that may not be helping us.

The Bible Journey: How to Read the Bible (OH302) – Justin Young – Because the Bible is a big book with a big message, we may be so overwhelmed that we don’t know where to start. Join us as we take small steps in learning how to understand this big book.

Speaking Boldly with God: What Moses Teaches Us about Prayer (OH303) – Jessica Lee – When life feels overwhelming or when it seems like God has abandoned us, it can be difficult to know how to pray. Moses felt all of those things, and he spoke with God face to face. Come learn from Moses how we can speak boldly with God in the midst of discontent.

Journey through Persecution and Suffering (OH306) – Elliot Lee – When Christians live in the broken, imperfect world, we may go through persecution, suffering, and depression. How should we deal with them wisely? What does the Bible say about them?
