Questions

Standing Firm the Wrong Way Genesis 37

  1. Warm-up: Do you know anyone who’s a tattletale or a teacher’s pet? Was that you, perhaps?
  2. Bible: What is Joseph’s relationship with his brothers like?
  3. Bible: What did Joseph share to his brothers?
  4. Bible: What are the sons of Jacob like in character?
  5. Understanding: Was there anything wrong what Joseph said? So why did his brothers respond in such a way?
  6. Understanding: Do you think Joseph understood how his brothers felt?
  7. Difference: How do we react to betrayal from friends or those close to us?
  8. Difference: What hope do we have when tragedy befalls us?
  9. Application: What can we do when we are going through a senseless or seemingly irreparable situation?
