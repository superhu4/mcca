Small Group “Helps”

As a Small Group we want to
  • Share, listen, ask questions, reflect, seek Jesus & pray.
  • Encourage & help one another to bond and grow with Jesus and each other.
  • Keep our sharing in this group. Keep things confidential.
  • Show respect and kindness to each other. Make it a safe space.
  • Laugh and enjoy.
  • Be aware of time and the amount of time we share so everyone has adequate time to share.
